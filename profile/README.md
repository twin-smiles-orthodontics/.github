## About

Twin Smiles Orthodontics is a women-owned orthodontic practice in Palo Alto, California, founded by twin sisters Drs. Amelia and Sydney Stoker. We provide comprehensive orthodontic care for children, teens, and adults — including traditional braces, clear aligners (Invisalign), early-treatment interceptive care, and adult orthodontics.

This GitHub organization holds the practice's internal engineering, operations, and digital-infrastructure repositories. It is **not** where patient-facing content lives — for that, please visit [twinsmilesortho.com](https://twinsmilesortho.com).

---

## What lives here

The repositories in this organization are private and used by the practice's small internal team to manage:

- 📝 **Content** — blog drafts, content strategy, and the like
- 🛠️ **Operations & IT** — vendor records, network documentation, runbooks, license tracking
- 📊 **Analytics** *(future)* — practice metrics, reporting, dashboards

If you've reached this page expecting a patient resource, you're in the wrong place — head over to [twinsmilesortho.com](https://twinsmilesortho.com) instead.
